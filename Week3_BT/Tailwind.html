Margin & Padding

4) m-number

<div class="m-5">...</div>

m-5 (margin all sides)
  • mt-8 → top
  • mb-8 → bottom
  • ml-8 → left
  • mr-8 → right
  • mx-8 → left + right
  • my-8 → top + bottom
mx-8 my-4

5) p-number

<div class="p-8">...</div>

p-8 (padding all sides)
  • pt-6 → top
  • pb-6 → bottom
  • pl-6 → left
  • pr-6 → right
  • px-6 → left + right
  • py-6 → top + bottom
py-8 px-4 mx-4
